Engineering4Youth

Objectives:

  • To help students develop the skills of practical application of mathematics, science and engineering
  • To teach students how to design a system, component or process to meet realistic constraints including economic, environmental, social, political, ethical, health and safety, manufacturability and sustainability
  • To encourage understanding and exploration of contemporary issues to build solutions that meet the desired needs
  • To help students develop an understanding of the professional and ethical responsibility of designing a system, component or process
